Davido Announces New Album

Date: July 9, 2026

Nigerian Afrobeats superstar Davido has announced that he is working on a brand-new album. Fans across social media are excited and are eagerly waiting for the official release date.

The singer said the album will feature both Nigerian and international artists, promising an exciting mix of Afrobeats, Amapiano, and Afro-fusion sounds.

More details will be shared as they become available.
