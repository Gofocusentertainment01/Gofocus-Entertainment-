Rescued Oyo Principal Reveals Why Teacher Was Beheaded by Kidnappers

Published by Gofocus Entertainment
July 14, 2026

The principal of Community High School in Orire Local Government Area of Oyo State, Mrs. Rachael Alamu, has revealed the heartbreaking circumstances surrounding the deaths of two teachers who were abducted alongside colleagues and pupils.

According to the principal, Mathematics teacher Mr. Michael Oyedokun was beheaded on the second day of the abduction, while another teacher, Deacon Esiyan Adegboye, was later killed during the period of captivity.

"They killed them purposely because they believed it would force the government to provide whatever they wanted."

Mrs. Alamu explained that the kidnappers intentionally carried out the killings to pressure the government into responding to their demands. She added that those in captivity initially believed they would all survive before the tragic events unfolded.

Despite the painful loss, the principal expressed gratitude to security agencies, government officials, local volunteers and everyone who contributed to the successful rescue of the remaining teachers and pupils.

The incident has once again raised concerns over insecurity affecting schools in Nigeria, with many citizens calling for stronger action against kidnapping and banditry.
